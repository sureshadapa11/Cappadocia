Our Story

A Hidden Gem on the A12

Tucked away at Capel St Mary just off the A12, Cappadocia Restaurant brings the warmth and richness of authentic Turkish cooking to Suffolk. Whether you are making the journey from London or exploring the local area, we offer a genuine taste of Turkey that keeps guests coming back.

Our kitchen is proud to serve everything from freshly grilled kebabs and hand-rolled lahmacun to hearty sharing platters and indulgent desserts like homemade baklava. Every dish is prepared with care, using quality ingredients and time-honoured recipes.
